Negative recommendation ruleback to ToC

This rule infers dissatisfaction of a survey based on its overall rating. If the overall rating of a survey is less than 4, it is inferred to have "Low" satisfaction saying Not to recommendation.

Survey c(?s) , hasRating dp(?s,?rating) , lessThan(?) -> hasRecommendation dp(?s,"false"^^boolean)

Positive recommendation ruleback to ToC
